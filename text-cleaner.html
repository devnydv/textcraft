Everything the Text Cleaner handles

One tool, eight cleaning operations. Select the ones you need and run them together.

⎵ Extra Whitespace

Collapses multiple consecutive spaces into a single space throughout your text, instantly fixing copy-paste spacing issues.

⬜ Blank Lines

Removes empty or whitespace-only lines between paragraphs, giving your text a clean, tight structure ready to use anywhere.

◁▷ Trim Lines

Strips leading and trailing spaces from every single line — perfect for cleaning up exported data, code, or pasted lists.

</> Strip HTML Tags

Removes all HTML tags and converts entities like &amp; to plain text characters automatically.

©™ Special Characters

Removes non-standard Unicode symbols, control characters, and punctuation that cause problems in databases or plain-text fields.

" " Smart Quotes

Converts curly "smart" quotes, em-dashes and ellipses to their plain ASCII equivalents for use in code or simple editors.

⏎ Line Breaks

Joins all lines into a single continuous block of text — great for un-wrapping paragraphs copied from PDFs or emails.

⇥ Tabs to Spaces

Replaces every tab character with a space so your text looks consistent across every editor, web form, and application.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Text Cleaner free to use?▾

Yes, completely free. No account, subscription or credit card required. All tools on TextCraft are free and will stay that way.

Does my text get sent to a server?▾

No. All cleaning operations run entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. Your text is never uploaded, logged, or stored anywhere.

Can I use this to clean text copied from a PDF?▾

Yes. Enable "Remove extra spaces", "Trim line spaces" and "Remove line breaks" to quickly turn broken PDF copy-paste into clean, readable prose.

Will removing special characters affect my content?▾

It removes non-ASCII symbols and control characters, keeping regular punctuation intact. Preview the output pane before copying to confirm the result looks right.

Is there a character limit?▾

No hard limit. Because everything runs locally in your browser, performance depends on your device. Most modern machines handle hundreds of thousands of characters without any issue.
