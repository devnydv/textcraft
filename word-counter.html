Common questions

How does this word counter work?
The tool processes your text directly in your browser using JavaScript. Words are counted by splitting the text on whitespace, sentences by detecting ending punctuation (. ! ?), and paragraphs by counting double line breaks. Nothing is sent to a server.
Is there a character or word limit?
No hard limit — you can paste full essays, reports, books, or any length of text. The tool handles it all in real time.
How is reading time calculated?
Reading time is estimated at 238 words per minute (the average adult reading speed), and speaking time at 150 words per minute — both widely accepted averages.
Does it count characters with or without spaces?
Both! The tool shows total character count (with spaces) and a separate count excluding spaces — useful for platforms that count characters differently.
Is this tool free?
Yes, completely free. No account, no sign-up, no ads, no tracking. Part of the TextCraft suite of free browser-based text tools.
